SparkSol was born from running two restaurants in Bangalore. Every solution we offer is something we built, tested, and use daily at our own outlets.

The story

We couldn’t find technology that actually worked for our restaurants. So we built it ourselves.

We run two restaurants in Bangalore — Hamza Express (biryani and ghee rice) and Nawabi Chai House (Irani chai cafe, open 24 hours). Between the two outlets, we built 39 APIs, deployed 609 code updates, and created systems for everything from WhatsApp ordering to kitchen displays to daily profit tracking.

When other restaurant owners saw what we built, they asked: “Can you set this up for me?”

That’s SparkSol. We don’t sell software you need to learn. We set up systems that work, hand them over, and support them. You get the results. We handle the technology.

Why only restaurants and cafes

Not salons. Not gyms. Not retail shops. Just food businesses.

Because we ARE a food business. We know what happens during Friday dinner rush. We know that your cook doesn’t care about “digital transformation.” We know that your real problem is cash not matching at end of day, not “needing a SaaS platform.”

Every solution we build comes from a problem we faced ourselves. That’s why it works.
