# SparkSol — Explainer Pages Plan

## Purpose
Each service gets a dedicated "how it works" explainer page with REAL VISUALS showing exactly what the customer and owner experience. Not pricing pages — visual walkthroughs.

## Page Template (every explainer follows this)

```
1. HERO
   "How [Service] Works for Your Restaurant"
   1-line: See exactly what happens, step by step.

2. CUSTOMER/USER JOURNEY (3-6 steps)
   Each step: Screenshot + caption
   Shows the EXACT flow from the user's perspective

3. WHAT THE OWNER SEES
   Dashboard/admin view screenshots
   "This is what you see on your phone/screen"

4. BEFORE vs AFTER
   Visual comparison of life without vs with this service

5. THE NUMBERS
   One clear stat or comparison that makes the value obvious

6. CTA
   Link to the service page (pricing/bundles) + WhatsApp
```
