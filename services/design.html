Common questions

What file formats do I get?
You get print-ready PDFs (CMYK, 300dpi), PNG files for digital use, and editable Figma source files so you can make small text changes yourself later. If your printer needs AI/EPS files, we include those too.
Do you handle printing?
We design, you print. We recommend Bangalore-based printing vendors we’ve worked with and share exact specs (paper weight, finish, dimensions) so the printer gets it right the first time.
Can I add festival designs later?
Yes. Festival campaign sets (5 social posts + 3 stories themed for Ramadan, Diwali, etc.) are ₹5,500 per campaign. These use your existing brand kit so everything stays consistent.
