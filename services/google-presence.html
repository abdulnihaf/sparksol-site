Common questions

Can’t I set up Google Maps listing myself?
You can. But 70% of restaurant listings have wrong hours, no photos, and no menu. We do it properly — 30+ photos, full menu, Q&A, review QR codes — in 5 days.
How do you get more reviews?
We place QR code standees at your tables and counter. When a customer scans it, they go straight to your Google review page. Your staff learns when to prompt — after the food arrives, not when they’re leaving.
How much do Google Ads cost?
You set the budget — we recommend minimum ₹10,000/month for restaurants. A click costs ₹5-25 in Bangalore. One new dine-in customer typically spends ₹300-500, so the return is 10-20x.
Why limited to 3 restaurants per area for ads?
If we manage Google Ads for 10 restaurants in the same area, they compete against each other and ad costs go up for everyone. By limiting to 3, your ad budget works harder.
How long before I see results?
Your listing appears within 5 days. Reviews start growing within 2-3 weeks. Google Ads bring customers from day 1.
