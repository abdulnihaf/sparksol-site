Common questions

What if my customers prefer ordering on Swiggy?
Keep Swiggy for discovery — new customers find you there. But once they’ve ordered, move them to WhatsApp for reorders. Most restaurants keep Swiggy for new customers and use WhatsApp for repeat orders. You don’t have to choose one or the other.
Do customers need to download an app?
No. They already have WhatsApp. They message your number, see your menu in a catalog, add items, and pay. Nothing to download, no account to create.
What does each order cost me on WhatsApp?
About 2.4% payment gateway fee (Razorpay) on each paid order. On a ₹400 order, that’s about ₹10 — compared to ₹100 on Swiggy. Cash on delivery orders cost you nothing.
How do I update my menu?
Your yearly plan includes 5 menu changes per month — price changes, new items, removing items. Just message us on WhatsApp and we update it within 24 hours.
Can I send offers to all my past customers?
Yes, but only on the Marketing (W2) and Complete (W3) plans. The basic ordering plan handles incoming orders only. Marketing adds broadcast campaigns so you can send offers to everyone who’s ordered before.
