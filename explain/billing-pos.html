How Your Own Billing System Works

Your billing, your data, your system. No monthly rent to anyone. See exactly what happens from the first order to the daily sales report.

Step 1

Billing system installed on your tablet — products, categories, prices

We set up your billing system on your own tablet. All your dishes configured with categories (Starters, Mains, Beverages), prices, variants (Half/Full), and modifiers (Extra Raita, No Onion). Your staff opens the app and starts billing.

Step 2

Customer orders — tap items — bill generated with GST

Staff taps the items, selects payment method (cash, UPI, card), and generates the bill. Receipt prints with your logo, GST breakdown, order number, and payment details. Takes 10 seconds.

🧾
Bill Receipt — Branded with GST
Printed receipt: restaurant logo at top, order number, item-wise breakdown with quantities and prices, subtotal, GST (5%), grand total, payment method (UPI/Cash/Card), date and time. Professional receipt your customers can trust.

Professional receipt — GST, logo, order details

Step 3

Multiple counters — counter, captain, delivery all connected

One billing counter at the front. A captain taking orders on a phone. Delivery orders coming from WhatsApp. All connected to the same system. Every order goes to the kitchen. Every rupee is tracked.

🔗
Multi-Counter Setup — All Connected
Three billing points feeding into one system: Counter tablet (walk-in customers), Captain phone (table orders), WhatsApp orders (delivery). All orders visible in one place. Kitchen sees everything. Owner sees everything.

Counter + captain + delivery — one system, one view

Your data stays with YOU — not with Petpooja

With Petpooja or any rental billing system, they hold your data. If you stop paying, you lose access to your own sales history. With SparkSol, the system runs on YOUR infrastructure. You own every byte of data. Forever.

Your Data, Your Control
Your billing system runs on your own server. Sales data, customer history, product performance — all yours. Export anytime. No vendor lock-in. Stop paying SparkSol’s AMC? The system still works. The data is still yours.

Step 5

Daily sales report on your phone

Every day, you get a sales summary on WhatsApp — total revenue, number of orders, average order value, top-selling items, payment split (cash vs UPI vs card). You know exactly how the day went before you go to sleep.
