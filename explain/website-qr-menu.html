How Your Restaurant Website
& QR Menu Work

Customers scan a QR code at your table and see your menu on their phone. People find you on Google and land on your website. Here’s exactly what happens.

Step 1

QR code at every table — customers scan, see menu on phone

We design and print acrylic QR standees for every table. Customer sits down, scans with their phone camera — your full menu opens instantly. No app download. No waiting for a waiter to bring the menu. Photos, prices, categories — all there.

📱
QR Menu on Customer’s Phone
Customer’s phone showing your digital menu: categories on top (Starters, Biryani, Curries, Breads, Beverages), each item with photo, name, price. Scrollable. Fast loading. Works on any phone — no app needed.

Scan QR → full menu on their phone in 2 seconds

Step 2

Full website with menu, photos, ordering links, and Google Maps

Your own website on your own domain (e.g., yourrestaurant.com). Home page with your story, full menu with photos, gallery, contact page with Google Maps embedded, click-to-call, and click-to-WhatsApp buttons. Looks great on phones and desktops.

🌐
Restaurant Website — Live at Hamza Express
hamzaexpress.in showing: hero section with restaurant photo, menu section with categories, about section, location with Google Maps, phone number, WhatsApp ordering button, Instagram feed. Mobile-responsive. Loads in under 2 seconds.

Your website — your domain, your brand, zero hosting cost

Step 3

People find you when they search on Google

We set up the technical stuff that helps Google understand your restaurant — page titles, descriptions, structured data, sitemap. When someone searches “biryani Shivajinagar” or your restaurant name, your website shows up.

🔎
Google Search Result — Your Restaurant
Google search results page showing your restaurant website at top: title, description, star rating, address, hours. Google Search Console connected — you can see which search terms bring people to your site.

Your restaurant showing up on Google search

Step 4

Analytics — see where visitors come from

Google Analytics installed on your website. You see how many people visited, where they came from (Google, Instagram, WhatsApp), which pages they looked at, and which city they’re from. Monthly report delivered to you.

📊
Website Analytics — Live at Hamza Express
Analytics dashboard: 2,400 visitors this month. Sources: Google 45%, Instagram bio link 28%, WhatsApp 15%, Direct 12%. Top pages: Menu (62%), Home (25%), About (8%). Most visitors from Bangalore. Peak hours: 12 PM – 2 PM and 7 PM – 9 PM.
